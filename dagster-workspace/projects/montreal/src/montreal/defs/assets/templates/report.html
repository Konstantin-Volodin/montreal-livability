Montréal Livability Report

Livability scoring for the Montréal agglomeration. Every address is scored 0-100 on proximity to six amenity categories (grocery, transit, parks, bike network, schools, health), blended into a single weighted score and rolled up here by municipality and H3 grid cell. Toggle amenities to rebuild the map score; hover or click a municipality row to highlight it.
